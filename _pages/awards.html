National Merit Semi-Finalist, September 2024

The National Merit Scholarship Corporation (NMSC) conducts the annual National Merit Scholarship program, in which over 1.3 million juniors from about 21,000 high schools take the Preliminary SAT exam (PSAT), which serves as an initial screen of program entrants. The national-wide pool of semi-finalists, representing less than 1% of US High School Seniors, includes the highest scoring entrants in each state.
